Modernity VS Tradition

Osaka is a very modern place with the latest technology such as super fast outdoor metros which take you through the whole city in a couple of minutes. But if you actually take the time to travel a bit further out of the city you get to experience the true history and culture and how people used to live in Japan in the past.

Architecture

On the EXPO 2025 you really get to experience Osaka's next level architecture even the hotels are designed with a very one-of-a-kind design and unique features.

Travel In Osaka

Traveling in Osaka is a breeze with the local metro which will take you super fast through the outdoors all while being incredibly silent because in the Osaka metro local people take speaking loudly as an inssult. Lucky for you Osaka's metro is the only metro in Japan where you can find directions and information in English.

Osaka's Temples And Activities

Historically, Osaka has a lot of things to offer. When going there, you should definitely visit the local temples to experience the original feel of the city and how people used to live here in the past. You can learn about Osaka's traditional culture and rich history while taking some time to truly appreciate the beautiful view of the nearby nature and local architecture.

Osaka's Food

Osaka's food tastes incredible while also being one of the healthiest food's in the world which is a paradox in other country's. Osaka is widely known for its street food, especially Takoyaki (octopus balls) and Okonomiyaki (savory pancakes) while these food are incredibly healthy they also taste amazing. These fantastic dishes can be found in the busy streets of Dotonbori. Here you don't only experience the street food but also the atmosphere and the smell of local specialties made with recipes of small local businesses which contribute to the authenticity of the amazing food.
