Osaka: Best Travel Destination 2026

Responsible Travel Guide 2026

Trip To Osaka

When I first visited Osaka, I was immediately amazed by everything there. Immediately after we landed and got to our hotel, we went to experience the tight alleys and local food. Osaka's culture is truly impressive because they have a very healthy lifestyle and eat healthy food, yet the food they eat tastes amazing.

Modernity VS Tradition

Osaka is a very modern place with the latest technology, such as super-fast outdoor metros which take you through the whole city in a couple of minutes. But if you actually take the time to travel a bit further out of the city, you get to experience the true history and culture and see how people used to live in Japan in the past.

Vibrance

Architecture

When in Osaka, every few steps you take you see an eye-catching piece of architecture, and it is impossible not to notice that even the hotels are designed with a one-of-a-kind design and unique features. All buildings are tightly crammed together with small alleyways in between. Japanese people are experts at making fantastic-looking structures out of wood, and what truly interested me the most is their incredible attention to detail and precision needed to make such beautiful structures.

Travel In Osaka

Traveling in Osaka is a breeze with the local metro, which takes you super fast outdoors while being incredibly silent. In the Osaka metro, speaking loudly is considered disrespectful. Lucky for you, Osaka's metro is the only metro in Japan where you can find directions and information in English because of the "EXPO 2025," where all countries presented their culture and technology.

Osaka's Temples And Activities

Historically, Osaka has a lot to offer. When visiting, you should definitely explore the local temples to experience the original feel of the city and learn how people used to live. You can immerse yourself in Osaka's traditional culture and rich history while appreciating the beautiful views of nearby nature and local architecture. To truly experience the local culture, participate in some of the local rituals guided by experts.

Local Specialties

Local Traditions

People in Osaka enjoy their free time differently than in other countries. For example, they visit Japan's many onsen (hot springs), which often have water from volcanoes or underground sources, sometimes located in massive caves with beautiful views. I highly recommend experiencing them. Others participate in local festivals and dance performances led by universities. You should join in to experience these traditional activities and appreciate how unique they are compared to other countries.

Osaka's Food

Osaka's food tastes incredible while also being among the healthiest in the world, a paradox elsewhere. The city is widely known for its street food, especially takoyaki (octopus balls) and okonomiyaki (savory pancakes). While these foods are incredibly healthy, they also taste amazing. You can find them in the busy streets of Dotonbori, where you experience not only the food but also the atmosphere and aromas from small local businesses, contributing to authenticity.

Beauty Of Nature In Osaka

Nature in Osaka and its surroundings offers options for everyone. You can hike to see beautiful waterfalls or go fishing and chat with locals about why they enjoy living in Osaka.
